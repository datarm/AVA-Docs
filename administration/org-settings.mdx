## General Settings

<Steps>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Regional Settings">
    Configure timezone, language, and regional preferences
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email Configuration">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Branding">
    Customize logo and colors
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuration

```yaml
organization:
  name: "Your Company Name"
  domain: "your-domain.com"
  timezone: "America/New_York"
  language: "en-US"

  email:
    smtp_host: "smtp.company.com"
    smtp_port: 587
    from_address: "ava@company.com"
    use_tls: true

  branding:
    primary_color: "#431968"
    secondary_color: "#ad2a97"
    logo_url: "/images/company-logo.png"
```
