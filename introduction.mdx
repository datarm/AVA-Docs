## What is AVA?

AVA (AI Virtual Assistant) by DataRM is an enterprise-grade AI-powered platform that revolutionizes how organizations manage data governance and risk management. Built for security-conscious enterprises, AVA provides intelligent, automated solutions for data discovery, classification, access control, and compliance tracking.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Intelligent Data Discovery">
    Automatically discover and catalog data assets across your entire organization with AI-powered classification and tagging.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Automated Compliance">
    Ensure GDPR, CCPA, SOC2, and HIPAA compliance with automated policy enforcement and audit trails.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Real-time Risk Scoring">
    Continuously monitor and assess data risks with machine learning-powered risk scoring and automated alerts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enterprise-grade Security">
    Built on zero-trust architecture with role-based access control, encryption, and comprehensive audit logging.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
