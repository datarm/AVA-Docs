## Overview

AVA enables CCPA compliance through automated data discovery, consumer rights management, and comprehensive audit trails.

## CCPA Features

### Consumer Request Portal
Enable consumers to submit privacy requests through self-service portal.

### Data Inventory
Maintain complete inventory of California resident data.

### Opt-Out Management
```bash
# Record opt-out preference
ava ccpa opt-out \
  --consumer-email "consumer@example.com" \
  --categories "marketing,analytics"
```

### Compliance Reporting
Generate CCPA compliance reports for regulatory requirements.
