- Addressing common concerns

## Key Rollout Phases

1. **Planning & Preparation**: Define goals, success criteria, timeline
2. **Pilot Launch**: Small group of early adopters
3. **Feedback & Iteration**: Gather insights, refine approach
4. **Phased Rollout**: Gradual expansion to departments
5. **Organization-Wide**: Full deployment with ongoing support
