## Getting Started with Adoption

<Steps>
  <Step title="Plan Your Rollout">
    Define timeline, pilot users, and success criteria
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train Champions">
    Identify and train power users who can help others
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Pilot">
    Start with a small group before org-wide rollout
  </Step>
  <Step title="Gather Feedback">
    Collect user feedback and iterate
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scale Organization-Wide">
    Roll out to all users with ongoing support
  </Step>
</Steps>
