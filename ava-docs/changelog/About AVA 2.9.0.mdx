# AVA 2.9.0

**Release Date:** November 15, 2025

## Overview

AVA 2.9.0 introduces advanced AI-powered data classification, enhanced risk scoring algorithms, and comprehensive DORA compliance features. This release focuses on automation, regulatory compliance, and enterprise-scale performance improvements.

---

## New Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI-Powered Data Classification Engine">
    - **Automated Data Discovery**: Machine learning models automatically identify and classify sensitive data across structured and unstructured sources
    - **Custom Classification Models**: Train organization-specific classifiers using your own data patterns
    - **Confidence Scoring**: Each classification includes a confidence score for audit and review purposes
    - **Real-time Classification**: Classify data as it enters your systems with sub-second latency
    - **Multi-language Support**: Enhanced NLP capabilities for 40+ languages
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="DORA Compliance Framework">
    - **ICT Risk Management**: Built-in templates and workflows aligned with EU Digital Operational Resilience Act
    - **Third-Party Risk Assessment**: Automated vendor risk scoring for critical ICT service providers
    - **Incident Reporting**: DORA-compliant incident classification and reporting workflows
    - **Testing Framework**: Structured approach to operational resilience testing
    - **Regulatory Dashboards**: Real-time compliance status monitoring
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Advanced Risk Scoring Engine 3.0">
    - **Multi-dimensional Risk Analysis**: Evaluate risk across 12+ dimensions including data sensitivity, access patterns, and threat intelligence
    - **Predictive Risk Modeling**: AI-driven predictions of emerging risks based on historical patterns
    - **Custom Risk Weights**: Configure risk calculation weights specific to your organization
    - **Risk Trends**: Visualize risk evolution over time with interactive charts
    - **Automated Risk Remediation**: Suggested actions based on risk severity
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Knowledge Search with Vector Embeddings">
    - **Semantic Search**: Find conceptually similar content even without exact keyword matches
    - **Multi-modal Search**: Search across documents, images, and structured data simultaneously
    - **Search Analytics**: Track search patterns to identify knowledge gaps
    - **Relevance Tuning**: Customize search ranking based on organizational priorities
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
