# Release Notes for AVA Update 2.7.1

Welcome to AVA 2.7.1—this release builds on your feedback and recent innovations to make connecting your data (and getting work done) easier than ever!

---

### **Highlights**

- **Unified Microsoft @ Mentions:** Most Microsoft-related @ mentions are now under one roof—`@Microsoft 365 Apps`. Manage calendar, email, Teams, and To-Do with a single mention.
- **@Jira Integration:** Say hello to `@Jira` mentions! Interact with your Jira issues directly from chat and tasks.
- **Microsoft To-Do Support:** `@Microsoft 365 Apps` now lets you reference Microsoft To-Do tasks for streamlined personal organization.
- **Multiple @ Mentions:** Use multiple @ mentions at once in chats and tasks! (Knowledge search corpora remain limited to one at a time, for now.)
- **Files & Images + @ Mentions:** Attach files and images alongside your @ mentions for fully contextual conversations.
- **Smarter Default Mentions:** `@Web` and `@Microsoft 365 Apps` are now automatically enabled in new chats—easily customizable in Settings.

---

### **Settings & Insights**

- **Connected Data Accordion:** Check out the revamped Settings! The new Connected Data accordion lets you:
  - Toggle whether @ mentions for Web and Microsoft 365 Apps are preselected in new chats
  - View all Connected Data sources you can access
  - Authenticate directly with Jira from your settings
- **Enhanced Usage Tracking:** Select customers can now benefit from more detailed usage stats, thanks to expanded metadata tracking.

---

### **Improvements**

- **Supercharged Knowledge Search:** Substantial enhancements to speed and accuracy, making it easier to find relevant info in large datasets.
- **Bug Fixes:** We squashed a handful of bugs—ideally, you’ll never even notice.

---

Your feedback shapes every release—keep it coming! There’s more to come, so stay tuned for what’s next. 🚀
