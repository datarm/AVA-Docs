## Overview

AVA integrates seamlessly with Salesforce to provide comprehensive governance and risk management for your CRM data.

## Prerequisites

<Info>
  - Salesforce administrator access
  - Ability to create Connected Apps
  - API access enabled
</Info>

## Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Connected App">
    In Salesforce Setup, navigate to **App Manager** and create a new Connected App
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure OAuth">
    Enable OAuth settings with required scopes: `api`, `refresh_token`, `offline_access`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Credentials">
    Copy Consumer Key and Consumer Secret
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect in AVA">
    ```bash
    ava connect salesforce \
      --name "Production Salesforce" \
      --instance-url "https://your-instance.salesforce.com" \
      --client-id "YOUR_CONSUMER_KEY" \
      --client-secret "YOUR_CONSUMER_SECRET"
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Discovered Data

AVA automatically discovers:
- Standard and custom objects
- Fields and field metadata
- Record counts and data samples
- User access and permissions
- Sharing rules and visibility
